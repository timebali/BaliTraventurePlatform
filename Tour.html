Tour Description

The Ubud Full Day Tour takes you through the cultural heart of Bali. Visit iconic landmarks such as the Sacred Monkey Forest, Tegalalang Rice Terraces, and Ubud Art Market. Immerse yourself in the rich traditions and natural beauty of Ubud.

Tour Highlights

  • Visit the Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary
  • Explore the Tegalalang Rice Terraces
  • Shop for souvenirs at Ubud Art Market
  • Enjoy a traditional Balinese lunch
  • Learn about Balinese culture and history

Itinerary

08:00 AM - Pickup

Pickup from your hotel in Bali.

09:30 AM - Sacred Monkey Forest

Explore the famous monkey sanctuary.

12:00 PM - Tegalalang Rice Terraces

Visit the stunning rice terraces and enjoy the view.

01:30 PM - Lunch

Enjoy a traditional Balinese lunch.

03:00 PM - Ubud Art Market

Shop for unique souvenirs and handicrafts.

05:00 PM - Return

Drop-off at your hotel.
