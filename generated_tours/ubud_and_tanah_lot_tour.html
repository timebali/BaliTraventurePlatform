Tour Description

Ubud and Tanah Lot Tour is a one day tourist trip to see the beauty of the tourist destination in Ubud village and continue to enjoy the beauty of the sunset at Tanah Lot Temple. We have designed and designed tour packages for those of you who want to see the highlights of Ubud and Tanah Lot villages in one day. The trip starts in the morning, where our tour driver will pick you up at the hotel you are staying at, make sure you have eaten breakfast beforehand, the first destination will be to visit Tegenungan waterfall which is a natural tourist spot to see the beauty of the waterfall in Ubud village, after that continue to Tegalalang rice terrace to see the beauty of the rice fields with terraces which are often very enchanting, continue to visit the Ubud Monkey Forest, a natural tourist spot where we will visit a small forest with a habitat for tame monkeys and see the beauty of the unspoiled forest, continue to the restaurant to enjoy lunch with delicious Indonesian food, after lunch the journey continues to Taman Ayun Temple to see the beauty of the temple with its enchanting garden and see the holy place for Hindus here, continue to Tanah Lot Temple to see the beauty of the sunset on the beach and see The uniqueness of Tanah Lot Temple is located on a rock on the beach. After that we will take you back to the hotel you are staying at with a very impressive experience on a one day trip to Ubud and Tanah Lot, the trip will be even more enjoyable with our professional and friendly driver.
